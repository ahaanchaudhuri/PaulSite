About Me

Hey everyone. My name is Paul Miguel Costa and I'm originally from a coastal city in Portugal called Figueira da Foz. I've been riding motorcycles for as long as I can remember and it has become a thrilling hobby that I share with my dear friends. We've gone on plenty of trips over the years and I wanted to document them in the form of scenic photos and smiling selfies. Welcome to my happy place.
